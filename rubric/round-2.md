# Rubric Design — Round 2: Cross-Critique

## Key Movements
- Gemini moved from 5 to **7 dimensions** (adopted GPT's citation split, proposed "pipeline" framing)
- GPT held at **8 dimensions** but tightened definitions, folded in Gemini's AI-specific lens (dead links, hallucinated URLs)
- Opus leaning toward **7-8 dimensions** with AI-native focus

## Near-Consensus Dimensions (all 3 agree these belong)
1. Claim Accuracy & Factual Reliability
2. Evidence Traceability & Link/Citation Integrity
3. Source Quality & Appropriateness
4. Analytical Synthesis & Insight
5. Epistemic Honesty / Uncertainty / Conflict Resolution
6. Task/Prompt Fidelity (+ Coverage)
7. Organization / Information Architecture

## Remaining Disagreement
- **7 vs 8**: Gemini argues 7 is MECE; GPT argues 8 gives better evaluation sensitivity
- **Coverage + Task Fidelity**: Gemini merges them (both punish missing sub-topics); GPT keeps separate (prompt adherence ≠ research judgment)
- **Decision Utility**: GPT keeps it in Task Fulfillment; Gemini says it's topic-dependent

## Gemini's "Pipeline" Framing
1. THE PROMPT: Task Fidelity & Scope Mastery
2. THE SEARCH: Source Quality + Traceability & Link Integrity (2 dims)
3. THE REASONING: Factual Reliability + Analytical Synthesis + Epistemic Honesty (3 dims)
4. THE OUTPUT: Information Architecture & Signal Density

## GPT's Revised 8
1. Claim Accuracy & Factual Reliability
2. Evidence Traceability & Citation Integrity
3. Source Quality, Diversity & Relevance
4. Analytical Synthesis & Insight
5. Coverage & Research Judgment
6. Uncertainty Handling & Conflict Resolution
7. Prompt Fidelity & Decision Utility
